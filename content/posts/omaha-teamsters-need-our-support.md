Teamsters Local 554 has been on strike against their employers Premier Midwest Beer & Beverage. Workers have been on strike since February 2 after the company:

- Refused to bargain in good faith
- Pushed major changes to long-standing healthcare coverage
- Proposed significant cost increases for workers and their families

These workers are prepared to fight to protect the contractual standards they have bargained for and restore the respect they deserve.

Let’s back them up!
